Frequently Asked Questions

Click on a question to view its answer

What are the temple visiting hours?

Our temple is open Monday to Friday from 6:00 AM to 11:30 AM and 5:30 PM to 8:30 PM. On Saturdays, we're open from 6:00 AM to 12:00 PM and 5:30 PM to 9:00 PM. On Sundays, temple hours are 6:00 AM to 11:30 AM and 5:30 PM to 8:30 PM. Special festival timings may vary and will be announced on our events page.

How can I book a special pooja?

You can book special poojas through our online Seva booking system. Simply navigate to the Sevas page, select the desired service, and follow the instructions. Alternatively, you can visit the temple office during open hours.

Is parking available at the temple?

Yes, we have a dedicated parking area for bikes that can accommodate up to 20-30 vehicles and there is no parking for cars. During major festivals, additional parking arrangements are made nearby with shuttle services to the temple.

Can I make donations online?

Yes, we accept online donations through our Donations page. You can contribute to various temple initiatives, programs, and development funds. All online donations are secure and tax-deductible.

How can I volunteer at the temple?

We welcome volunteers for various activities including festival preparations, community services, and educational programs. Please contact the temple office for more information.
